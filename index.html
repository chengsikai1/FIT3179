California Wildfires

California is one of the places having the most deadliest and destructive wildfire seasons.This visualisation helps to generate insights on what locations in California are under fire threat, what time do Wildfires usually occur and how to exextinguish.

Wildfires Distribution

The proportional symbol map on the right give us a better insight of the distributions of wildfires from 2013 to 2019 in California as well as the area of land burned in that fire represented by size of the circle. We could know wildfires happened frequently in which area of california. It is obvious that there ware most fires in 2017 by selecting different years.

Months that wildfire happens

A commom opinion is that summer is a period of higher chance for wildfire occurs due to the temperature and humidity. The data indeed indicates that most wildfires occured in June, July and August from 2013 to 2019.

Equipments improvement

Due to advancements in wildfire handling technology, lower number of deployed personnel can handle larger and expansive of wildfires as evident from the years 2013 and 2018 where crews deployed in 2013 is more than 2018 althrough much larger number of wildfires occured in 2018 . A similar conclusion can be drawn for the number of water tenders involved.
